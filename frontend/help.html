Help & Support

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book an appointment?

To book an appointment, click on the "Book" button in the navigation menu, fill in your details, select your preferred salon, choose a service, date, and time slot.

Can I cancel or reschedule my appointment?

Yes, you can cancel or reschedule your appointment up to 24 hours before the scheduled time. Please contact the salon directly or use the contact form for assistance.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept all major credit/debit cards, UPI payments, and cash payments at the salon.

How do I know if my booking is confirmed?

You will receive a confirmation email and SMS with your booking details once your appointment is confirmed.

Booking Guide

  1. Click on "Book" in the navigation menu
  2. Enter your personal details
  3. Select your preferred salon
  4. Choose the service you want
  5. Pick a convenient date and time
  6. Confirm your booking
